Wellcome Subidkhali Rokeya Khanam Girls Pilot Secondary school

Subidkhali Rokeya Khanam Girls Pilot Secondary school is situated at Mirzagonj Upazila Head Quarter under district Patuakhali beside Bakergonj Barguna Highway. It was established in 1972. It was one of the renowned educational institutions in the upazila for girls.
It was founded by Late Jennat Ali Khan Ex CharmanDeulySubidkhali Union Parishad ,Mirzagonj.The School got 1st recognition from Intermediate and Secondary Education Board, Jessor .By that time it had humanities group. It opened science group in1982, commerce group in 1997 and vocational curriculum 1999
